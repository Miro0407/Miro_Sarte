Biography

Kobe Bryant in full name Kobe Bean Bryant, is known as a superstar basketball player because of his incredible skills and winning shots created in game. He also won 5 NBA championship rings and 2 olympic gold. He is born on August 23, 1978 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and died on January 26, 2020, in Calabasas, California. His wife name is Vanessa Bryant and they have four daughters (Natalia Diamante, Gianna Maria-Onore, Bianka Bella, and Capri Kobe).
